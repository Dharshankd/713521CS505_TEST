About

SNS College of Technology is one among the premiere institutes in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India. It was established in 2002 as part of the SNS Groups. The college is approved by AICTE and affiliated to the Anna University, Coimbatore. Anna University, Chennai confirmed the conferment of Autonomous status to SNS College of Technology for a period of 10 years with effect from 2018–19 to 2027–28.
